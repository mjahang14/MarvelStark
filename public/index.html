Drone Path Finder

Today, the biggest challenge that most enthusiast and hobbyist face is to fly drone in an allowed/safe air space i.e. to avoid no flying zone(NFZ). Our solution will not only enable drone to avoid NFZ but also empower it to fly independently, with on the fly route calculation algorithm, between two GPS points in space while avoiding NFZ. We will develop an additional layer of No Flying Zone basis some criterion. This layer will be juxtaposed on top of google maps or any other maps based on GPS coordinates. Our algorithm will then determine and calculate the best possible to fly drone, automatically avoiding no flying zones precinct(a GPS co-ordinates boundary). This path will then be layered onto google map. This information will be passed to drones, which will act as route for drone.
